About GovLinkChecker

GovLinkChecker is a user-friendly website designed to assist in checking the authenticity of links. Its primary goal is to help users determine if a given link belongs to a trusted site approved by the Singapore government, relying on a whitelist of authorised domains.

It's important to note that GovLinkChecker is a personal project and is currently not endorsed or supported by the Singapore government. Keep in mind that the site may indicate a link as trusted as long as it contains a whitelisted domain, even if the link is invalid.
