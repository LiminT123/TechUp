About GovLinkChecker

GovLinkChecker is a user-friendly website designed to check the authenticity of links. It aims to help users determine if a given link belongs to the Singapore government or its associated partners, relying on whitelisted domains and a list of trusted sites.

It's important to note that GovLinkChecker is a personal project and is currently not endorsed or supported by the Singapore government. Keep in mind that this site does not check the validity of the link, it only verifies its domain.
